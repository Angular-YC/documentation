Troubleshooting

Build fails on SSR bundle Verify @angular/ssr setup and ensure server entry compiles under the selected Angular version.
Manifest mismatch in Terraform Regenerate with angular-yc build and re-upload artifacts before applying infrastructure.
Stale rendered responses Check response-cache TTL and purge endpoint auth headers/signature handling.
